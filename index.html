Discover a Home Built on Trust


Refined homes for meaningful living

With a legacy of designing spaces that blend purpose with poetry, PCPL has always believed that a home should be more than just four walls—it should be an experience. Every project is a testament to craftsmanship, thoughtful architecture, and an unwavering commitment to creating spaces that enhance life’s most cherished moments. With exclusive 3BHK residences available, Gulnar rises above the suburb, offering breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and the Bandra skyline. Designed for those who appreciate life beyond the rush, it is more than just a home it is a space where time slows, where conversations linger, and where every moment between milestones is just as meaningful as the milestones themselves.

 Amenities 

Our amenities are created to provide a harmonious balance of comfort, recreation, and lifestyle convenience. Each space is purposefully designed to offer residents an experience that enriches daily living and inspires moments of calm, joy, and connection.
