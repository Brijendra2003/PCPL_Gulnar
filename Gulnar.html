Refined homes for meaningful living

Smart layouts, serene views and over 15 lifestyle amenities.

Gulnar presents thoughtfully designed 2 & 3 BHK apartments in Bandra West, crafted to elevate everyday living through smart architecture, spacious layouts, and a seamless connection to nature. Set within one of Mumbai’s most sought-after neighbourhoods, the project blends modern design with lush open spaces, creating a balanced environment where comfort, privacy and community living come together effortlessly.
